About

Ever get frustrated programming, yeah me too. Well this is a site built around user generated rage comics. They are meant to share frustrations and have a good laugh.

Adding Your Own

You can submit a rage comic by simply clicking the Submit a Rage button at the top and uploading a comic. We will run them through our moderation queue to make sure they are in some way related to developers.

You can generate your own rage comics on many different sites we tend to use Rage Maker. They are simple, fun and easy to make.

Authors and the Site

The idea for Developer Rage came from Luke Crouch. He handed the idea off to me, Buddy Lindsey, and I decided to run with it a bit to see what would happen.

If you would like to help develop the site further you are welcome to submit pull requests to the developer rage github repository since it is open source.
